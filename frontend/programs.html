SAVE THE EDUCATION SOUTH SUDAN ORGANIZATION PROGRAMS

 

Empowering underprivileged children in South Sudan through quality education and mentorship programs.

 
 




Educational Resources

Save the Education South Sudan Organization provides a wide range of educational resources to schools and educational institutions. These resources include books, textbooks, educational equipment, and other materials needed for a quality education. The organization aims to ensure that all students have access to these resources, promoting a culture of lifelong learning.

 
 

 
 




Awareness Campaigns

 

We conduct various campaigns and events to raise awareness about the importance of education and its role in shaping the future of South Sudan. These campaigns include workshops, seminars, and public discussions, aiming to engage parents, students, and the broader community in the importance of education.
