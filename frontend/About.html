WHAT DOES SAVE THE EDUCATION SOUTH SUDAN ORGANIZATION DO?

Daily sessions and interviews.

Our organization conducts daily sessions and interviews with children and youth in South Sudan to provide them with educational opportunities and support. These sessions are designed to address the unique needs and challenges faced by each individual, ensuring that they receive personalized attention and guidance. Through interactive discussions and hands-on activities, we aim to foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills that will help our participants excel in their academic and personal pursuits.

 
 

 
 





The teamwork and innovativeness of the Save The Education South Sudan Organization.

The Save the Education South Sudan organization prides itself on the teamwork and innovativeness of its staff and volunteers. Our team is composed of dedicated individuals who share a common goal of improving access to education in South Sudan. Through collaborative efforts and innovative strategies, we work together to address the complex challenges facing South Sudan's educational system. Our team members bring a variety of skills and experiences to the table, including teaching expertise, fundraising abilities, and a commitment to social justice. By leveraging our collective resources and creativity, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of children and youth in South Sudan.
